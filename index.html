Our Story

A Kitchen Built on Memory

Luciana's Table was born from a single wooden recipe box that Luciana Moretti carried from Abruzzo to Portland in 1998. Inside were her mother's instructions for pasta dough, the ratio of Pecorino to pepper, and a note that read: "Feed people well and they will never forget you."

Her daughter Marta now runs the kitchen, and her son Paolo manages the dining room. Together they've grown their mother's vision into one of Portland's most treasured dining destinations — while keeping every dish exactly as she made it.

We make our pasta fresh every morning. We braise our meats low and slow. We source from the farmers and cheese makers we trust. And we close on Mondays so we can rest and share a meal as a family.
